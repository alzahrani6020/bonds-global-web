Case Study

Plastic Factory: 30% Cost Reduction

A mid-sized plastic factory in Riyadh was suffering from accumulated dead stock and rising unplanned maintenance costs. Profit margin had dropped below 8%.

Diagnosis: Raw material costs were 18% above average due to lack of consolidated supplier negotiations. There was also material loss during production due to improper machine calibration.

Intervention: We restructured the supplier list, negotiated annual fixed-price contracts, and designed a preventive maintenance schedule. We also analyzed the cash cycle and shortened collection periods.

Result: Total costs reduced by 30% within 6 months, and profit margin rose to 19%.
