📖 How to Read the Results

🟢 Excellent (>50%): The dish is highly profitable. It easily covers fixed costs and leaves a healthy net profit.

🟡 Acceptable (40-50%): The dish is profitable but the margin is tight. Consider raising the price or reducing costs slightly.

🔴 Danger (<40%): The dish is unprofitable or has a very weak margin. Review pricing immediately.

💡 Formulas:

Platform Commission = Price × Commission Rate
Total Prime Cost = Food Cost + Packaging
Net Profit = Price − Commission − Total Cost
Profit Margin = (Profit ÷ Price) × 100
