Key Metrics to Understand

🥘

Food Cost %

Percentage of ingredient cost relative to selling price. Successful restaurants keep it below 30%.

📦

Contribution Margin

Profit per dish after deducting ingredients, packaging, and commission. Must cover fixed costs.

🎯

Break-Even Orders

Daily orders needed to cover all costs. Less than 80 orders/day = feasible.
