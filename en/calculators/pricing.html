How Does the Calculator Work?

We use internationally recognized pricing formulas in managerial accounting.

📐 Mathematical Formulas

Total Cost = Direct Cost + Overhead Cost
Selling Price = Cost ÷ (1 − Desired Profit Margin)
Net Profit = Profit − Tax − Zakat

⚠️ Warnings & Clarifications
