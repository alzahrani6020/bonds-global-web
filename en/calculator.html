How does the calculator work?

We use the internationally recognized break-even formula from cost accounting.

📐 Mathematical Formulas

Break-Even (units) = Fixed Costs ÷ (Selling Price − Variable Cost)
Break-Even (amount) = Break-Even (units) × Selling Price
Net Profit = (Quantity × Selling Price) − Fixed Costs − (Quantity × Variable Cost)
