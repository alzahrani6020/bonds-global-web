Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to what our clients most commonly ask about financial consulting and the services we offer.

How much does the financial consultation cost?
The first consultation is free. After that, we send a custom quote based on the scope of service and your business sector. We prefer transparent pricing tailored to each client individually.
How long does the consultation or project take?
The discovery session takes 30–60 minutes. Financial projects (analysis, budgeting, feasibility) range from two weeks to two months depending on data complexity and company size.
Which sectors do you serve?
We serve all sectors: trade and retail, manufacturing, tourism and hospitality, agriculture and food, professional services, real estate and construction, technology and software, and healthcare.
Is the consultation online or in person?
Sessions are held online via Zoom or Google Meet by default. If you are in Cairo or Riyadh, an in-person meeting can be arranged upon coordination.
Is my financial data confidential?
Yes. We adhere to strict confidentiality. Your data is protected by non-disclosure agreements and is never shared with any third party. Your business confidentiality is our top priority at every stage.
What payment methods are available?
We accept bank transfers, Vodafone Cash (for clients in Egypt), and Mada/electronic payments (for clients in Saudi Arabia). Installment options are also available depending on project size.
What is your refund policy?
If work has not yet started, you are eligible for a full refund. If analysis has already begun, a 50% refund applies based on the completed stage.
How do I book my first consultation?
Click "Book Your Consultation," fill out the contact form, or reach out to us directly via WhatsApp and we will schedule an appointment within 2–3 business days.
Do you guarantee investment returns or financing approval?
No. We provide analysis and consulting based on your actual data and information; the investment decision and outcomes remain your full responsibility.
