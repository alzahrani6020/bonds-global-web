Details

Our Services in Detail

Financial Analysis

Reading financial statements, liquidity and profitability indicators, and comparing actual vs. budgeted performance to identify strengths and weaknesses with precision.

  • Income statement & balance sheet analysis
  • Profitability, liquidity & debt indicators
  • Actual vs. budget comparison

Cash Flow Management

Cash inflow and outflow mapping, payment prioritization, and early warning of liquidity shortages before they impact your business.

  • Cash flow forecasting
  • Cash deficit financing plan
  • Collection and payment cycle optimization

Budgeting & Forecasting

Realistic monthly and quarterly budgets with actual vs. planned comparison and performance monitoring indicators.

  • Annual & quarterly budget preparation
  • Cost & revenue estimation model
  • Budget variance / surplus reports

Specialized Feasibility Studies

Clear cost and revenue estimates with risk analysis before expansion or a major investment decision — tailored to every sector.

  • Factory & industrial feasibility
  • Hotel & tourism project feasibility
  • Farm & agricultural project feasibility
  • Commercial & real estate feasibility

Risk Analysis

Identifying potential financial, operational, and market risks, evaluating their probability and expected impact.

  • Financial & credit risk assessment
  • Market & competition risks
  • Risk mitigation plans & alternatives

Financial Strategy

Simple KPIs, clear investment priorities, and a short-term implementation plan focused on results.

  • Defining financial performance KPIs
  • Investment & expansion priorities
  • Short & medium-term action plan

Operational Consulting

Simplifying procedures, distributing roles, and raising team efficiency to reduce costs and improve productivity.

  • Process reengineering
  • Role & responsibility distribution
  • Operational performance measurement

Surveys & Market Research

Designing scientific surveys and conducting market research to support your investment and expansion decisions with accurate data.

  • Customer & employee survey design
  • Customer satisfaction & loyalty studies
  • Market research before product or branch launch
  • Competition & market trend analysis
