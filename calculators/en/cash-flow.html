How Does the Calculator Work?

📐 The Formulas

Total Inflows = Sales Revenue + Other Income + Loans Received
Total Outflows = Rent + Salaries + Raw Materials + Marketing + Taxes + Loan Repayments + Other Expenses
Net Cash Flow = Total Inflows − Total Outflows
Closing Balance = Opening Balance + Net Cash Flow

⚠️ Disclaimers

  • This is an estimation tool and not a substitute for professional financial advice.
  • Results depend on the accuracy of input data.
  • Make sure to include all recurring obligations.
  • A negative balance in any month indicates a need for additional financing.
