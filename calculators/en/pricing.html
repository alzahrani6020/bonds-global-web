How does the calculator work?

We use internationally recognized managerial accounting pricing formulas.

📐 Mathematical Formulas

Total Cost = Direct Cost + Overhead
Selling Price = Cost ÷ (1 − Desired Margin)
Net Profit = Profit − Tax − Zakat
