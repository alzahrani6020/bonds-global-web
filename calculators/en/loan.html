How does the calculator work?

We use the internationally recognized mathematical formula for loan amortization.

📐 Mathematical Formulas

Installment = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n − 1]
P = Net Loan | r = Periodic Rate | n = Number of Payments
Effective Annual Rate = (1 + r)^periods_per_year − 1
